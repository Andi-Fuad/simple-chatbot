Q: What is Bangkit Academy?
A: Bangkit Academy is a career readiness program that aims to produce high-caliber technical talent for world-class Indonesian technology companies and startups, fully supported by Google, GoTo, Tokopedia, and Traveloka. Upon program completion, students will possess the technical proficiencies, soft skills, and English language proficiency to transition seamlessly from academia to the professional sphere and thrive at leading companies. Lecturers will also gain the technical expertise and high-quality materials essential for success in research.
